ScratchAddons

Hi there! Welcome to ScratchAddons! ScratchAddons is an open source project started by World_Languages aimed at creating a collection of browser extensions, userscripts and userstyles and combining them into one browser extension. It has two major motives: to help improve the look, feel, and function of the scratch website, and to collect and archive userstyles and userscripts because the Scratch Team is deleting them. Eventually, ScratchAddons will be a browser extension for Chrome and Firefox, but for now it is being developed on GitHub.

ScratchAddons enhances the scratch experience greatly by adding many features, such as searching blocks, bug fixes, searching dropdowns in the editor, and much more!

ScratchAddons is being rapidly developed by a team of volunteers.

ScratchAddons is open source, so you can contribute to it easily, suggest features, chat with the developers, download the latest build and much more!
